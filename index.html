About Shrivardhan Valley

Welcome to Shrivardhan Valley gated community, which will transport you to an ideal world of its own.

Calling it "Destination- Happiness",Kherdi, an alluring coastal village, just 4km from Shrivardhan, is a haven of serenity and scenic beauty, basking in nature's abundance. Perched on a higher terrain, the club house is the central attraction, with a panoramic view of the entire complex & beyond.

Offering you a wealth of experience, this estate stretches across an expansive 17 acres with 15 acres of NA land. It is collector's approved plot in Konkan(Area 500sq onwards), with a separate 7/12 additional area for each plot, farm house plots, independent bungalows, row houses, right in the midst of a magnificent expanse of land.
