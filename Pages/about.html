About Us

Shrivardhan Valley at a Glance

Long beaches, warm days, twinkling nights and fairy lights:

A long weekend is around the corner. The familiar feeling of faraway beaches, that relaxing feeling of cool sands between your toes, drenching in the warm fuzzy morning sunlight, the salty breeze gentle brushing you and your favorite drink with the umbrella… What a life!!

Packing your bags

Pack your bags and head to the Punir, a coastal village, just 3-4 hours away from Thane. Your “Perfect Paradise” is not far away.

Luxury of space

While you can savor crisp air, enjoy the refreshing atmosphere, your farm house will become a reality with all its ample amenities for recreational and with a luxury of space.

Abundant Green

Half of the area is solely dedicated to green and open areas. Lending a sense of boundless stretch of land are the wide roads comprising a 50 feet main road and other 30 feet roads.

Something for everyone

There is something for every age group, liking and finesse, be it proximity to the beach, green spaces, senior citizen park, the modern club house, fun-filled kids play area, utterly enjoyable shopping complex, multi-cuisine restaurant, infinity pool and a whole lot more.
